Common Problems

KitchenAid Cooktop Issues We Fix

Gas Burner Clicking

Moisture, switches, electrodes, and spark modules commonly cause nonstop clicking and failed ignition.

Burner Not Heating

Electric surface elements, infinite switches, and wiring failures all cause dead cooking zones.

Weak or Uneven Flame

Burner head alignment, clogged ports, and gas-flow faults lead to poor heating performance.

Touch Controls Not Responding

Electronic cooktops can fail at the control panel, interface board, or power supply.
