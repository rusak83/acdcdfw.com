Common Problems

Dacor Issues We Fix Daily

Burner Won't Ignite

Igniter, spark module, or gas valve issue — the most common Dacor range complaint.

Oven Not Heating Evenly

Bake/broil element, temperature sensor, or convection fan. We diagnose the root cause.

Control Board Faults

Unresponsive touch panel or error codes on Modernist smart models.

Refrigeration Not Cooling

Condenser, evaporator fan, or sealed-system issue on built-in columns.

Dishwasher Not Draining

Drain pump, clogged line, or control fault. We carry common parts.

Error Codes / Alarm

We diagnose the underlying fault, not just clear the code.
