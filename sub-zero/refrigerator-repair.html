Common Problems

Sub-Zero Refrigerator Problems We Fix

Every issue has a root cause. Victor diagnoses it on-site — no guessing, no unnecessary parts.

❄️ Not Cooling or Warming Up

Dirty condenser coils, failed evaporator fan, thermostat issue, or sealed system problem. Most common Sub-Zero complaint — we see it daily.

💧 Water Leaking Inside or Under

Clogged defrost drain, failed door gasket, cracked drain pan, or water line connection. We find the source fast.

🔊 Loud Noise — Buzzing, Clicking, Knocking

Compressor starting issue, evaporator fan hitting ice, or condenser fan bearing failure. Don't ignore unusual sounds — early repair is cheaper.

🚨 Error Codes (EC 01–EC 50)

Sub-Zero error codes point to specific component failures. We read the code AND fix the root cause — not just clear it.

🧊 Ice Buildup on Evaporator

Defrost heater, defrost thermostat, or defrost timer failure. Requires manual defrost plus component replacement.

🚪 Door Not Sealing Properly

Worn or torn door gasket, misaligned hinges, or warped door panel. Affects cooling efficiency and compressor life.

🌡️ Temperature Not Holding

Thermostat or temperature sensor failure. Sub-Zero should hold within 1–2°F of setpoint.

💡 Display Not Working

Control board failure or display panel issue. Common on older BI series units and 700-series models.
