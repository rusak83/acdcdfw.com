Common Problems

Sub-Zero Issues We Fix Daily

Not Cooling Properly

Condenser coils, evaporator fan, thermostat or sealed system issue. Most common Sub-Zero complaint.

Ice Maker Not Working

Water inlet valve, ice maker module, or frozen water line. We carry common parts.

Loud Compressor Noise

Clicking, humming, or knocking from the compressor. Early diagnosis saves major cost.

Error Codes / Alarm

EC 01–EC 50 codes. We diagnose the root cause, not just clear the code.

Water Leaking

Clogged defrost drain, door gasket failure, or water line issue.

Frost / Ice Buildup

Defrost system failure — heater, thermostat, or timer. We fix the cause, not the symptom.
