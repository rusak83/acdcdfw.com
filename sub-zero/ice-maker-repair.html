Common Problems

Sub-Zero Ice Maker Problems We Fix

Sub-Zero ice makers have multiple failure points — water supply, inlet valve, module, and freezer temperature all affect output. Victor isolates the exact issue.

🧊 Not Making Ice At All

Frozen water supply line, failed water inlet valve, failed ice maker module, or harvest arm in off position. Victor checks all four on the first visit.

🔲 Small, Hollow, or Thin Cubes

Insufficient water supply — low water pressure, partially clogged inlet valve, or failing solenoid. Easily diagnosed and fixed on-site.

🦠 Ice Tastes Bad or Has Odor

Overdue water filter (replace every 6 months), stale ice bin, or food odors absorbed. Victor checks filter status and recommends replacement if needed.

🌊 Water Leaking from Ice Maker

Failed water inlet valve not closing fully, cracked water line, or clogged drain in the ice maker compartment.

🐌 Ice Production Too Slow

Freezer temperature too warm, low water pressure, or partially clogged filter. Sub-Zero should produce a full bin in 24 hours.

🧊 Ice Cubes Clumping Together

Ice bin not used frequently enough, bin heater failure, or partial melt-and-refreeze cycle from temperature swings.

🔊 Noise During Ice Making

Water hammer from inlet valve, ice dropping noise (normal), or grinding from a failing harvest motor.

❌ Ice Maker Not Cycling

Failed thermostat, harvest heater issue, or control board problem. The ice maker module stops triggering the harvest cycle.

Sub-Zero Ice Maker Repair FAQ

Four most common causes: (1) frozen water supply line in the door — common in DFW summer when cold air meets humid entry; (2) failed water inlet valve — the solenoid stops opening; (3) failed ice maker module — the harvest cycle doesn't trigger; (4) harvest arm switched to off position accidentally. Victor checks all four on the first visit.

Typical repair costs: water inlet valve $200–$350, ice maker module $300–$500, water line repair $150–$300, full ice maker assembly replacement $400–$700. Water filters should be changed every 6 months — $50–$80 for the filter itself. The $90 diagnostic fee is waived when you proceed with repair.

Sub-Zero recommends every 6 months, or when the filter indicator light turns on. In DFW, hard water accelerates mineral buildup — change it on schedule. An overdue filter reduces ice production and affects taste. Victor can replace it during any service visit.
