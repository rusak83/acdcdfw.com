Common Problems

Sub-Zero Freezer Problems We Fix

Sub-Zero's dual refrigeration system means freezer and fridge issues are often independent. Victor pinpoints the exact failed component on-site.

🌡️ Not Freezing or Running Warm

Evaporator fan failure, defrost system issue, or refrigerant leak. Sub-Zero's dual system usually isolates the problem to the freezer side.

🧊 Frost Buildup on Walls or Shelves

Defrost heater burned out, bi-metal thermostat failed, or defrost timer stuck. Frost builds until the evaporator is completely blocked.

🚪 Door Not Sealing

Torn or compressed door gasket lets warm air in continuously — causes frost, temperature swings, and overworks the compressor.

🔊 Loud Noise from Freezer

Evaporator fan hitting ice buildup, failing fan motor bearing, or compressor knock. Don't wait — noise gets worse and damage spreads.

🚨 Error Codes / Alarm Beeping

Sub-Zero alarm indicates a temperature deviation over 18°F. We read the full error log to identify the root cause, not just silence the alarm.

🌡️ Temperature Swings

Thermostat or temperature sensor failure. A Sub-Zero freezer should hold within 2°F of setpoint consistently.

💡 Display or Controls Not Responding

Control board failure common on older BI and 700-series units. We diagnose board vs. keypad to avoid unnecessary replacement.

💧 Water Pooling in Freezer

Clogged or frozen defrost drain. Melt water has nowhere to go — builds up until it freezes solid or leaks out the bottom.
