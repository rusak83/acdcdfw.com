Common Problems

Thermador Range Problems We Fix

🔥 Burner Not Lighting

Clogged burner ports, failed igniter electrode, faulty igniter switch, or bad module. Victor tests each component and replaces only what failed.

🔁 Continuous Clicking

Moisture in igniter switch after boil-over, or a failed module. Don't ignore — a stuck igniter that won't stop is a safety concern on gas ranges.

🌊 Weak Flame

Clogged burner ports reducing gas flow, or low gas pressure. Victor cleans burner ports and checks gas pressure on-site.

🔥 Oven Not Heating

Bake igniter (gas) or bake element (dual-fuel/electric) failure. Most common oven issue — Victor usually has the part on the truck.

🔲 One Burner Not Working

Individual burner igniter electrode, spark switch, or gas valve issue. Victor isolates the faulty component and replaces only that.

💨 Gas Smell Near Range

If you smell gas and burner isn't on, turn off gas at the valve and call immediately. Can be a leaking regulator or valve. Safety first — Victor diagnoses safely.
