Common Problems

Thermador Dryer Problems We Fix

Thermador dryers are precise, but heating circuits, airflow, rollers, and sensors still fail over time. Victor diagnoses the exact cause instead of guessing at parts.

🔥 Not Heating

Failed heating element, thermal fuse, thermostat, or control problem. If the drum turns but clothes stay wet, the heat circuit is the first place we test.

⏱️ Takes Too Long to Dry

Restricted venting, weak blower output, dirty moisture sensors, or low heat output. Two-cycle drying is usually an airflow or sensor problem, not normal wear.

🔄 Drum Not Spinning

Broken drive belt, seized idler pulley, failed motor, or worn rollers. A humming dryer with no drum movement needs service before the motor overheats.

🔊 Loud Screeching or Thumping

Roller wear, idler pulley failure, or blower wheel damage. Noise is usually the warning sign before a no-spin failure.

🌡️ Overheating or Shutting Off

Restricted airflow, failed thermostat, or a clogged vent can overheat the unit and trip safety devices. We test airflow, outlet temperature, and sensor response.

📲 Sensor or Display Issues

Moisture sensor faults, dead buttons, or Home Connect/control board issues can stop the dryer from completing cycles correctly.
