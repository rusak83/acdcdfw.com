Common Problems

Thermador Ice Maker Problems We Fix

Thermador ice makers are integrated into high-end refrigerators — when they fail, they often point to a deeper system issue. Victor finds the root cause, not just the symptom.

🧊 Not Making Ice

Frozen water supply line, failed water inlet valve, or ice maker module failure. In Freedom Collection fridges, freezer temp above 10°F stops production entirely.

⬜ Small or Hollow Cubes

Restricted water flow from a clogged filter (RWF01 or WF50 — replace every 6 months) or low water pressure. Victor checks filter status and water pressure on every visit.

🤢 Ice Has Bad Taste or Odor

Overdue water filter, stale ice in the bin, or food odors from the fridge compartment contaminating ice. Filter replacement plus bin cleaning resolves most taste complaints.

💧 Ice Maker Leaking

Cracked water fill tube, faulty water inlet valve not closing fully, or ice bin overflow. Leaks can damage the fridge interior — needs prompt diagnosis.

⏱️ Making Ice Too Slowly

Weak water inlet valve, partially clogged filter, or freezer running slightly warm. Thermador Freedom Collection ice makers cycle roughly every 90 minutes — slower means something is restricting the process.

🧱 Ice Clumping in Bin

Ice clumping means the freezer temperature is cycling too warm, melting and re-freezing cubes. Usually a freezer temperature or defrost issue — Victor checks both during service.
