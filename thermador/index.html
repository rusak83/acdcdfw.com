Brand Expertise

Why Thermador Requires a Specialist

Thermador engineering is premium — and proprietary. Not every technician knows it.

⭐

Star Burner Technology

Thermador's 5-tip Star Burner uses a unique igniter configuration not found on any other brand. Igniter replacement, spark module diagnosis, and gas valve calibration require brand-specific knowledge. Generic parts won't fit.

🧊

Dual-Compressor Refrigerators

Freedom Collection column refrigerators use independent dual-compressor systems — one for fresh food, one for freezer. Diagnosing which compressor is failing, and how the two circuits interact, requires hands-on Thermador experience.

🔌

Home Connect & Smart Features

Thermador's Wi-Fi connected appliances (Home Connect platform) add an electronics layer above the mechanical system. Error codes, connectivity resets, and control board faults require both electrical and software diagnostic skills.

🛡️

ExtraLow Simmer System

Thermador's ExtraLow burner uses a dual-valve mechanism to achieve a true 100°F simmer — lower than any competitor. When it fails, the repair involves the secondary valve and bypass circuit, not a standard gas repair.

Common Problems

Thermador Issues We Fix Daily

Star Burner Won't Light

The 5-tip Star Burner requires the proprietary igniter to fire all 5 tips simultaneously. Moisture, food debris, or a failed spark module causes incomplete ignition. We diagnose and fix this on the first visit.

Oven Not Reaching Temperature

Bake igniter (gas) or bake element (electric) failure. Also: convection fan motor failure causes hot spots. Thermador Professional ovens have a dual convection system — one fan failure throws off baking significantly.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Condenser coils, evaporator fan, thermostat, or compressor failure. Freedom Collection columns — dual compressor failure requires identifying which unit is affected before ordering parts.

Dishwasher E15 Error

E15 = water detected in the base pan. Thermador's anti-flood system shuts down the machine. Requires draining the base, inspecting the float sensor, and identifying the source of the leak. Not a DIY repair.

Continuous Clicking (Burner)

Igniter stuck on — caused by moisture or a failed igniter switch. Common after boil-overs on the cooktop. Safe to use temporarily but annoying. We replace the switch and clean the igniter system.

Induction Zone Not Working

Freedom induction cooktops can display E-series error codes. Individual zone circuit board failure, touch control panel malfunction, or power inverter issue. We diagnose the specific zone affected.
