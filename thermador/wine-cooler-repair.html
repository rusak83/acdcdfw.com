Common Problems

Wine Cooler Problems We Fix

Whether it's a Sub-Zero wine column in a Thermador kitchen or another wine storage unit, Victor diagnoses and repairs these issues on-site.

🌡️ Not Holding Temperature

Failed compressor, dirty condenser coils, door seal failure, or thermostat malfunction. Temperature fluctuations above 2–3°F affect wine aging. Victor pinpoints the cause without parts guessing.

🔊 Loud Compressor

Compressor running harder than normal due to dirty coils, refrigerant issue, or failing compressor. Noise increases as the problem worsens — early diagnosis saves a costly compressor replacement.

📳 Vibration (Bad for Wine Aging)

Vibration disturbs sediment and disrupts the aging process. Causes include loose compressor mounting, internal shelf rattle, or unit not level. Victor checks mounting and leveling during service.

⚠️ Display Errors

Error codes on display panel indicate sensor failure, control board issue, or communication fault. Victor reads error codes and identifies the specific component — no unnecessary part replacement.

🥶 Over-Cooling

Wine cooler running too cold — thermostat stuck, control board malfunction, or stuck expansion valve. Over-cooling can cork wine by freezing and expanding the liquid.

💧 Humidity Problems

Low humidity dries corks; high humidity causes mold. Humidity issues are often caused by door seal failure or unit placement. Victor checks seals and advises on correct installation.
