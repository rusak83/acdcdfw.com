Common Problems

Thermador Refrigerator Issues We Fix

Most Thermador refrigerator failures follow predictable patterns. Here's what we see most often — and what's involved in the fix.

Not Cooling

Dirty condenser coils, failed evaporator fan, or compressor issue. On Freedom Collection dual-compressor models, we identify which compressor is at fault before recommending any repair. Often a condenser cleaning resolves the problem.

Ice Maker Not Working

Water inlet valve failure, frozen fill tube, ice maker module failure, or low water pressure. Thermador column fridges have the ice maker in the refrigerator section — a different configuration than typical models. We know the layout.

Water Dispenser Problems

Dispenser not working, slow flow, or leaking. Common causes: failed water inlet valve, frozen water line in the door, or a faulty dispenser actuator. We trace the water circuit from inlet to dispenser nozzle.

Error Codes (E-codes)

Thermador refrigerators display E10–E51 codes covering sensors, compressors, defrost heaters, and control boards. A reset clears the display but doesn't fix the root cause. We diagnose the underlying component failure behind each code.

Loud Compressor Noise

Clicking, banging, or continuous humming points to compressor wear, refrigerant pressure imbalance, or a failing start relay. On dual-compressor Freedom units, we test each compressor independently to identify the source.

Freezer Section Over-Freezing

Defrost system failure causes ice accumulation on the evaporator coil. This blocks airflow and leads to inconsistent temperatures across compartments. Defrost heater, thermostat, or defrost timer replacement resolves it.

Thermador Expertise

Freedom Collection: What Makes It Different

🔬

Dual-Compressor System

Freedom Collection 30", 36", and 48" column refrigerators use two independent compressors — one for the fresh food zone, one for the freezer. When one fails, the other may continue running normally. A generic technician may misread this and recommend full replacement. We test each circuit independently.

❄️

Column Evaporator Configurations

30" and 36" columns use a different evaporator layout than the 48" model. Accessing the evaporator for cleaning or replacement requires removing specific panel configurations that vary by column width. We know the exact disassembly sequence for each model.

📱

Home Connect Diagnostics

Wi-Fi enabled Thermador refrigerators log error history in the Home Connect app. Victor reviews the app error log during diagnosis — this often reveals intermittent failures that don't show up during a standard inspection, saving diagnostic time.
