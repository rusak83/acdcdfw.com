About ACDC Home Services

Premium appliance, refrigeration, and HVAC specialists serving Dallas-Fort Worth.

Who We Are

ACDC Home Services is a Dallas-Fort Worth repair shop focused on premium home appliances, commercial refrigeration, and residential HVAC. We work on the equipment most other shops avoid — Sub-Zero, Viking, Wolf, Thermador, KitchenAid, JennAir, GE Monogram, and Dacor — and we get it right the first time.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Specialists, not generalists. We invest in tools, parts knowledge, and training specifically for high-end and built-in appliances.
  • Written estimates before any work. No surprises. You approve the scope and the price before we turn a screwdriver.
  • 4.8★ from 430+ Google reviews. Our customers stay with us because we explain what we're doing and stand behind the work.
  • Same-day and next-day appointments. When your refrigerator stops cooling, you don't have a week to wait.
  • Commercial coverage. Restaurants, property managers, and multi-location operators get priority response and consolidated billing.
