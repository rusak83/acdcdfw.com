Common Problems

Viking Issues We Fix Daily

Burner Not Lighting

Spark igniter module, igniter electrode, or clogged burner ports. Most common Viking complaint — usually a quick fix.

Oven Not Heating

Bake igniter failure (gas models), bake element failure (electric/dual fuel), or oven control board issue.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Condenser coils, evaporator fan, thermostat, or sealed system issue. Victor diagnoses on-site.

Continuous Clicking

Igniter module stuck — can be dangerous. Caused by moisture, food debris in the switch, or a failed module.

Temperature Inaccuracy

Oven thermometer calibration, temperature probe, or control board drift. We calibrate to manufacturer spec.

Dishwasher Not Cleaning

Spray arm blockage, pump failure, or water inlet issue. Viking dishwashers should clean well — if not, it's fixable.
