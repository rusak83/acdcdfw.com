Common Problems

Viking Dishwasher Problems We Fix

🍽️ Not Cleaning Dishes

Clogged spray arms, failed wash pump, blocked filter, or low water pressure. Victor disassembles and tests each component to find the exact cause.

💧 Not Draining

Clogged drain hose, failed drain pump motor, or blocked air gap. Standing water after a cycle is always a repairable issue.

🚫 Not Starting

Door latch not engaging, door switch failure, control board issue, or thermal fuse blown. Victor tests the full start sequence on-site.

🚪 Door Not Latching

Door latch assembly failure or worn strike. If the door doesn't latch, the dishwasher won't start — a common Viking issue after years of use.

💦 Leaking Water

Door gasket failure, pump seal leak, or loose water inlet connection. Victor finds the source and seals it on the first visit.

🔊 Loud Noise During Cycle

Debris in pump, failing wash motor bearing, or spray arm hitting rack. Grinding or rattling is always a symptom — Victor finds the cause.
