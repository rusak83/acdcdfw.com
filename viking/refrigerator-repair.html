Common Problems

Viking Refrigerator Problems We Fix

Viking refrigerators are built to last — but when something fails, Victor finds the exact cause and fixes it on-site.

❄️ Not Cooling or Warming Up

Dirty condenser coils, failed evaporator fan, thermostat issue, or sealed system problem. Most common Viking fridge complaint in DFW.

💧 Water Leaking Inside or Under

Clogged defrost drain, failed door gasket, cracked drain pan, or water line connection issue.

🔊 Loud Noise — Buzzing, Clicking

Compressor starting issue, evaporator fan hitting ice, or condenser fan bearing. Early repair is cheaper.

🧊 Ice Buildup on Evaporator

Defrost heater, thermostat, or timer failure. Requires manual defrost plus component replacement.

🚪 Door Not Sealing

Worn door gasket or misaligned door. Reduces cooling efficiency and overworks the compressor.

🌡️ Temperature Not Holding

Thermostat or temperature sensor failure. Victor calibrates to manufacturer spec.
